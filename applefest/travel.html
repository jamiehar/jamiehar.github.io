Travel

Bus

The TCAT is Ithaca's local bus transit. Check out these sites and apps to plan a trip to Apple Fest by bus.

Buses are usually busy during Apple Fest, but there is virtually no limit to the number of people you can have in your travel group.

Cornell students: Bring your ID! Buses are free for all Cornell students on Saturdays and Sundays. If you have an unlimited student pass, just tap your ID and hop on. Otherwise, bus fares per adult are $1.50 or $2.50, depending on your zone.

Rideshare

If you want a more private, comfortable experience than the bus and are willing to spend a bit more, consider rideshare.

Ithaca has both Uber and Lyft as its local rideshare services, although Uber is more common than Lyft.

Prices will vary by time of day and distance, but you won't have to worry about parking.

Personal

If you or a friend wants to drive, plan your route with an interactive map. You might even choose to walk or bike instead, depending on your distance.

The only costs you might incur are for gas and parking, which vary by distance and location.
