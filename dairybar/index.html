The Cornell Dairy Bar is a great place to grab a cone or a snack from Cornell's own dairy processing plant. Located in the sparkling, renovated Stocking Hall, the Cornell Dairy Bar offers its unique ice cream, milk, yogurt, pudding, and Big Red Cheddar, along with exclusive merchandise. Perfect for families and groups of all ages, The Cornell Dairy Bar invites you today!
